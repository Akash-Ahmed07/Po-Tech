About Grow Easy

Empowering education through free and accessible learning resources

Our Mission

Po-Tech is dedicated to democratizing education by providing free access to high-quality educational resources. We believe that knowledge should be accessible to everyone, regardless of their financial situation or geographical location.

What We Offer

  • Free Books: Comprehensive collection of Bangladeshi and international books across all academic levels
  • Video Courses: Structured YouTube video courses for HSC and SSC students, organized by subjects and chapters
  • Blog Platform: Community-driven platform where students and educators can share knowledge and experiences
  • Responsive Design: Accessible across all devices - laptops, tablets, and smartphones

Our Vision

To become the leading platform for free educational resources in Bangladesh and beyond, fostering a community of learners who support each other in their educational journey.

Why Choose Grow Easy?

Quality Content

Curated educational materials from trusted sources and experienced educators.

Community Driven

Built by students, for students. Share knowledge and learn from peers.
